# Oficio Taller — Marketing & Communications

## About
Agent team that focuses on building, launching and iterating marketing campaigns that communicate the voice and vision of Oficio Taller.

## Key Principles
- Architecture sells through **sensibility + experience + intention**, not spectacle
- The right media understand **silence, context, and the human**
- Every publication gets a **distinct narrative angle** — never send the same press kit twice
- Think in different angles of the same project

## Available Skills

### `/press-kit`
Build personalized press kits for architecture/design media publications.

**Usage**: `/press-kit <project-name> [publication]`

**Publications configured**:
- `archdaily` — Complete technical documentation
- `yellowtrace` — Sensorial narrative
- `the-local-project` — Process + materials
- `architecture-hunter` — Visual-first Instagram curatorship
- `ad-mexico` — Luxury design with Latin American cultural lens
- `archdigest` — International design authority, editorial storytelling
- `yinjispace` — Atmospheric architectural photography curation
