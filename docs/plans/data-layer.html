Key Business Metrics
MetricDefinitionFormulaSource Tables
lead_to_contract_rate % of leads that become signed contracts COUNT(projects WHERE contract_signed_at IS NOT NULL) / COUNT(projects) dim_project
days_ingest_to_contract Calendar days from first contact to contract signature contract_signed_at − lead_received_at dim_project
avg_days_per_gate Average days Marcela review sits before a decision, per gate AVG(days_in_stage) GROUP BY gate_id fact_gate_decision
revision_rate_per_phase Average number of pass_to_agent decisions before approval, per gate AVG(revision_round) GROUP BY gate_id fact_gate_decision
fee_per_sqm Architecture fee per m² of designed area architecture_fee / total_design_m2 dim_project
budget_variance_pct % difference between original cost estimate and engineering-phase update (updated_estimate − base_construction_cost) / base_construction_cost × 100 dim_project + budget-alignment.json
fit_score_to_close_rate Correlation between avg fit score at DG-02 and eventual contract signing CORR(fit_avg_score, contract_signed_at IS NOT NULL) dim_client + dim_project
payment_collection_lag Days between milestone trigger and payment received, per milestone AVG(paid_at − triggered_at) GROUP BY milestone_id fact_payment
pipeline_velocity Active projects in each segment at a point in time COUNT(projects) GROUP BY awaiting_gate WHERE project_state = 'active' dim_project
bid_vs_estimate Contractor bids vs. Oficio's original cost estimate bid_total / (cost_per_sqm × total_design_m2) fact_bid + dim_project
