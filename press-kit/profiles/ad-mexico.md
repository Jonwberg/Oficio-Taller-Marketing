# Architectural Digest México y Latinoamérica

## Overview
- **Instagram**: @admexico (522K followers)
- **Website**: admagazine.com (AD México)
- **Audience**: Design-savvy, affluent readership in Mexico and Latin America
- **Format**: Print magazine + digital + social media
- **Reach**: Leading luxury design publication in Spanish-speaking world
- **Lists**: AD100 (top architects and designers)

## Editorial Focus
- **Luxury design and lifestyle** with a Latin American lens
- Architecture, interiors, art, design, and culture
- Projects that blend sophistication with regional identity
- Celebrity homes and designer showcases
- Design trends and cultural commentary
- Mexican and Latin American architecture with international standards
- Events coverage: Art Weekend GDL, IDD, Expo Osaka

## Voice & Tone
- Sophisticated, aspirational, culturally aware
- Bilingual sensibility (Spanish-first, internationally minded)
- Editorial authority — "La autoridad en diseño"
- Lifestyle-oriented: architecture as a way of living
- Celebrates Mexican and Latin American design identity

## Visual Requirements
- Magazine-quality photography (high production value)
- Styled interiors with curated objects and art
- Architectural photography with lifestyle elements
- Portraits of architects/designers
- Cover-worthy hero images
- Print resolution: 300 DPI minimum
- 15-25 images recommended

## Content Format
1. Feature article (1000-2000 words)
2. Project narrative connecting design to culture and lifestyle
3. Architect/designer profile and quotes
4. Styled photography with art direction notes
5. Material and furniture credits
6. Cultural context: art, landscape, local traditions

## What to Emphasize
- **Cultural identity**: How the project reflects Mexican/Latin American design sensibility
- Connection to art, craft, and local traditions
- Lifestyle narrative: how the space is inhabited
- Material luxury and refinement
- Designer furniture and curated objects within the space
- The story of the client and architect relationship
- Regional materials and techniques elevated to luxury

## What to De-emphasize
- Raw technical specifications
- Construction engineering details
- Unfinished or under-construction images
- Budget or cost discussions
- Overly minimalist descriptions without narrative

## Submission
- Editorial team contact through admagazine.com
- PR agencies often mediate submissions
- AD100 listing is a significant credential
