# ArchDaily

## Overview
- **Website**: archdaily.com
- **Audience**: Architects, students, construction professionals worldwide
- **Reach**: 18M+ monthly visitors, largest architecture website globally
- **Language**: English and Spanish (separate editions)

## Editorial Focus
- **Complete technical documentation** of projects
- Emphasis on architectural drawings: plans, sections, elevations, details
- Construction systems and material specifications
- Contextual analysis: site, climate, urban conditions
- Functional program and spatial organization
- Sustainability strategies and environmental performance

## Voice & Tone
- Objective, informative, technical
- Neutral journalistic tone — not promotional
- Facts-driven: dimensions, materials, systems
- Avoids poetic or overly subjective language

## Visual Requirements
- High-resolution images (minimum 2000px wide)
- **Mandatory**: floor plans, sections, site plan
- Construction details and diagrams valued highly
- Exterior and interior photography showing spatial qualities
- Before/after or construction process images welcome
- Image format: JPG, minimum 10-15 images recommended

## Content Format
1. Project data sheet (name, architect, location, area, year, photography)
2. Architect's description (500-1000 words)
3. Technical drawings
4. Photo gallery with captions

## What to Emphasize
- Structural innovation and material choices
- Relationship between program, site, and form
- Environmental strategies (ventilation, lighting, water management)
- Construction details and building systems
- Measurable data: areas, heights, material quantities

## What to De-emphasize
- Lifestyle or aspirational narratives
- Emotional or poetic descriptions
- Designer furniture or decoration details
- Client lifestyle stories

## Submission
- Submit via archdaily.com/submit
- Include all drawings and high-res photography
- Provide full project credits
- Projects must be built and completed
