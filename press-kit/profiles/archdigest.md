# Architectural Digest (Global)

## Overview
- **Instagram**: @archdigest (11.1M followers)
- **Website**: architecturaldigest.com
- **Tagline**: "The International Design Authority"
- **Audience**: Global, affluent, design-conscious readership
- **Format**: Print magazine + digital + video + social
- **Reach**: The most recognized design publication worldwide
- **Community**: 52.5K+ members in Architectural Digest channel

## Editorial Focus
- **Luxury architecture, interiors, and lifestyle at the highest level**
- Celebrity homes and designer showcases (Open Door video series)
- High-end residential architecture globally
- Interior design with art-world crossover
- Design authority — sets trends, defines taste
- Features: Lenny Kravitz, Kendall Jenner, Walton Goggins-level profiles
- Cultural commentary through design

## Voice & Tone
- Authoritative, polished, aspirational
- Editorial excellence — long-form features with depth
- Sophisticated storytelling that weaves design with culture
- Name-drops designers, artists, and cultural references
- Both accessible and elevated

## Visual Requirements
- **World-class photography** — commissioned or editorial quality
- Styled, art-directed interiors
- Portraits of architects/designers/homeowners
- Video content (Open Door series format)
- Print-quality resolution (300+ DPI)
- 20-30 images for a full feature
- Hero images that could be magazine covers

## Content Format
1. Long-form feature article (2000-3000 words)
2. Narrative-driven storytelling
3. Extensive quotes from designer and homeowner
4. Art-directed photo shoot
5. Furniture, art, and object credits throughout
6. Video tour component increasingly standard

## What to Emphasize
- **Star power**: the architect's reputation and vision
- Design provenance: custom furniture, art collection, designer pieces
- The story behind the home — who lives there and why
- Architectural ambition and innovation
- Cultural significance of the project
- Art and design objects within the space
- The architect-client creative relationship

## What to De-emphasize
- Technical construction details
- Budget or cost information
- Standard spec sheets
- Generic descriptions
- Small or modest-scale projects (unless architecturally significant)

## Submission
- Subject line: "PROJECT SUBMISSION" (in caps)
- **Exclusivity is critical** — project should NOT be published elsewhere
- When accepted, AD sends its own photographer and writer (7-month process)
- Identify the right editor for your project type (home tour vs. product vs. emerging designer)
- Digital presence matters: editors will check website and social media
- Response time: 2-4 weeks; follow up politely by email only
- PR representation recommended but not required
