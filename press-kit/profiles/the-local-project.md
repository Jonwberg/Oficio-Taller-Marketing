# The Local Project

## Overview
- **Website**: thelocalproject.com.au
- **Audience**: Design-conscious audience interested in process, materials, and place
- **Base**: Australia, with growing international coverage
- **Reach**: Strong digital presence, podcast, video content

## Editorial Focus
- **Process + materials** — how things are made, not just how they look
- Deep dives into design process and decision-making
- Material sourcing, selection, and application
- Relationship between architecture and place/landscape
- Residential architecture with emphasis on livability
- Australian and international projects with strong sense of place

## Voice & Tone
- Thoughtful, measured, intimate
- Values depth over spectacle
- Process-oriented — interested in the "why" and "how"
- Respectful of craft and tradition
- Conversational but substantive

## Visual Requirements
- High-quality photography showing material qualities
- Process and construction documentation valued
- Images of materials in raw and finished states
- Landscape and site context shots
- Interior details showing joinery and craftsmanship
- Video/film content increasingly important
- 15-20 images recommended

## Content Format
1. Project overview connecting design to place
2. Detailed material palette with sources
3. Process narrative: design evolution, challenges, discoveries
4. Architect interview or Q&A format
5. Construction details and craft documentation
6. Video tour or walkthrough (bonus)

## What to Emphasize
- **Material journey**: sourcing, selection, why each material was chosen
- Design process: sketches, iterations, pivots
- Relationship to site and landscape
- Craft and construction techniques
- Collaboration with artisans and builders
- How the project evolved from concept to completion

## What to De-emphasize
- Finished "hero shots" without context
- Generic architectural descriptions
- Awards and accolades
- Square footage and price points
- Styled/staged photography without authenticity

## Submission
- Contact via website
- Process documentation is a significant differentiator
- Video content gets priority
