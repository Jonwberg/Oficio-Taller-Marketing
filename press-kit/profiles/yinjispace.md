# Yinji Space

## Overview
- **Instagram**: @yinjispace
- **Focus**: Architecture photography curation
- **Aesthetic**: Warm, atmospheric, contemplative architecture
- **Reach**: Growing community of architecture photography enthusiasts
- **Base**: International, with strong presence in contemporary residential architecture

## Editorial Focus
- **Atmospheric architectural photography** — spaces that breathe
- Contemplative, quiet architecture
- Projects that prioritize human experience and material warmth
- Strong connection between architecture and landscape
- Residential and hospitality projects with strong spatial poetry
- Earth tones, natural materials, warm palettes
- Mexican and Latin American contemporary architecture featured regularly

## Voice & Tone
- Minimal, poetic
- Let the images speak
- Contemplative and quiet
- Values restraint over spectacle
- Culturally curious — features projects globally

## Visual Requirements
- **Atmospheric, warm-toned photography**
- Golden hour and natural light
- Images that show spatial depth and layering
- Material textures visible and prominent
- Interior-exterior threshold moments
- Furniture and objects that feel lived-in, not staged
- Hammocks, textiles, ceramics — tactile elements
- 5-10 carefully curated images per carousel

## Content Format
1. Photo carousel (Instagram carousel format)
2. Brief project information: location, architect, photographer
3. Minimal text — atmosphere over explanation
4. Focus on spatial moments, not project documentation

## What to Emphasize
- **Spatial poetry**: thresholds, transitions, framed views
- Warm materiality: wood, stone, earth, concrete with warmth
- Natural light: how it enters, moves, and transforms spaces
- Architecture that feels like a landscape
- Moments of rest and contemplation within spaces
- The dialogue between built form and nature

## What to De-emphasize
- Technical drawings or plans
- Project statistics or specifications
- Sharp, clinical photography
- Overly styled or cold interiors
- Text-heavy content
- Corporate or commercial projects

## Submission
- DM or tag on Instagram
- Photography quality and mood are the selection criteria
- Projects that align with their warm, contemplative aesthetic get featured

## Press Kit Angle for Oficio Taller
→ **Lead with atmospheric moments**: a hammock in golden light, a framed view of landscape
→ Select images that feel warm, quiet, and contemplative
→ Show thresholds between interior and exterior
→ Highlight earth materials: exposed concrete, wood, stone, clay
→ Minimal text — let the architecture breathe
→ This is the perfect outlet for Oficio Taller's sensibility: silence, context, the human
