# Segment C — Ana (Area Program)

Ana built the 337sqm area program for Casa Vista from the seed data, distributing space across 12 categories: 4 bedrooms (20sqm each), 4 bathrooms (9sqm each), kitchen (25sqm), dining/living (55sqm), family room (30sqm), office (15sqm), pool (40sqm), 2 terraces (20sqm each), and service areas (16sqm). Ana noted in assumptions that the HOA's 7m height limit constrains the massing to a two-story scheme with no three-story configuration.
