# Segment I — Ofelia (Contractor Bidding)

Ofelia managed the bidding process for Casa Vista, sending the bid package to two contractors: Constructora Los Cabos S.A. de C.V. (USD 590,000 — development pre-approved, 13 months) and BCS Builders Group (USD 635,000 — faster timeline, 11 months, pre-approval pending). Both bids are within the client's USD 400K–700K budget range. Recommendation: Constructora Los Cabos — lower cost, verified HOA pre-approval, and lower compliance risk.
